Our Mission

Rooted in Purpose, Driven by Nature

Green Bharati Foundation was born from a deeply held belief that India's environmental legacy must be actively protected and regenerated — not just preserved in policy documents, but lived out in forests, coastlines, ports and cities.

We work at the intersection of environmental science, community action, and institutional collaboration — partnering with government bodies, port authorities, PSUs, and civil society to drive measurable green impact across the Indian subcontinent.

Our approach combines ground-level plantation drives, mangrove restoration, coastal cleanup, research, and capacity building into a unified mission: more green, less pollution, stronger ecosystems.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Partner With Us Through CSR

As a freshly launched Section 8 company, Green Bharati Foundation is actively seeking its first CSR partnerships — especially in plantation, forest creation, and coastal greening initiatives.

Founded by Shri Rajashree Dabke — a serving senior government officer with decades of honest public service — Green Bharati Foundation is positioned as a credible, transparent, and mission-driven partner for corporates looking to make real environmental impact through their CSR spend.

We are a registered Section 8 company with a clearly defined Objects Clause, making us eligible to receive CSR funds under Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013 — specifically under items relating to environmental sustainability and ecological restoration.

📋 CSR Eligibility under the Companies Act, 2013

Green Bharati Foundation's activities qualify under Schedule VII, Item (iv) — "ensuring environmental sustainability, ecological balance, protection of flora and fauna, animal welfare, agroforestry, conservation of natural resources and maintaining quality of soil, air and water" — and Item (x) — "rural development projects." Companies spending on our plantation and forest creation projects may count these contributions towards their mandatory CSR spend under Section 135 of the Companies Act.

Dedicatee & Inspiration

Shri Rajashree Dabke

Founder · General Manager (Finance), JNPT Port Mumbai

Green Bharati Foundation was founded by Shri Rajashree Dabke — a distinguished officer currently serving as General Manager (Finance) at JNPT Port Mumbai, and a lifelong embodiment of honest, non-corrupt public service.

Having served across multiple of India's most strategic major port authorities, Shri Dabke gained firsthand witness to the urgent need for greening, coastal protection, and environmental care in industrial and port zones — a conviction that led her to establish this Foundation.

Her decades of principled government service, spanning Kandla, Visakhapatnam, Cochin, and JNPT, reflect a career built on integrity and dedication — values she now channels into building a greener, healthier India through the Foundation she founded.
